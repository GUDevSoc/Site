Controls

Player 1

  • A, D - Move around
  • W - Jump (Note: you can jump twice in the air before touching a surface, and walljump)
  • Left Shift - Dash

Player 2

  • Left Arrow, Right Arrow - Move around
  • Up Arrow - Jump (Note: you can jump twice in the air before touching a surface, and walljump)
  • Right Shift - Dash
