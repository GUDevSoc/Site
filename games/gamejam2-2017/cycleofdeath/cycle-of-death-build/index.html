--- layout: unityplayer title: Cycle Of Death contributors: Peter, Phill, Regina, Sara description: Two player game arena game where you fight to kill or ressurect zombies. Created in Unity for the GUDEV GameJam in October 2017. ---

Controls

Player 1 - HUNTER

W,A,S,D - Move Up, Left, Down, Right E - Use Freeze> ability (can only be used when you have some mana) As the hunter, you want to kill zombies by walking into them Your Freeze ability locks the necromancer in place for a bit

Player 2 - NECROMANCER

Arrow Keys - Move Up, Left, Down, Right Right Shift - Use Haste ability (can only be used when you have some mana) As the necromancer you want to awaken zombies by walking over graves Your Haste ability doubles the speed of all awakened zombies (THIS STACKS)
