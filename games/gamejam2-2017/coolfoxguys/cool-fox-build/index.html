--- layout: unityplayer title: Cool Fox Guys contributors: Nicola Mari, Stefano Mercadante, Lukas Knapek, Francisco Macia description: Live an incredible underwater platformer adventure featuring the Fox Guys (although one of them is a Wolf). Use their unique powers to complete the level full of deadly traps. Created in Unity for the GUDEV GameJam in October 2017. ---

Controls

Player 1 - Wolf

A,D - Move left, right W - Jump Ctrl - Interact (DO NOT JUMP WHILE INTERACTING) As Wolf, you can walk through green doors

Player 2 - Fox

Left Arrow, Right Arrow - Move left, Right Up Arrow - Jump Alt - Interact As Fox, you can double jump (press jump while in midair)
