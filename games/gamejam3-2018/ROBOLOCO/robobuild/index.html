Controls

Plug in XBOX controllers for this game - it won't work otherwise

Right Analogue Stick - Move Robot A - Jump Left Trigger - Eat scrap Left Bumper (Hold down) - Compress scrap into box Right Trigger - Fire Absorbed Scrap
