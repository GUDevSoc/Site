--- layout: unityplayer title: Ocean contributors: Faiz Atif, Peter Maltzoff, Anna Blackwell, Ramandeep Mungur and Lukas Knapek description: Survive in a post apocalyptic sand ocean, and salvage scrap to make your SAND BOAT stronger! Created in Unity for GUDEV GameJam 3 in March 2018. ---

Controls

  • W,S - Move forward, backwards
  • A, D - Turn Left, Right
  • Q, E - Fire Left Cannons, Right Cannons
  • Spacebar (when over oil/scrap) - Salvage (to get better weapons or health)
