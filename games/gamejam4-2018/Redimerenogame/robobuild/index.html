Controls

Plug in controllers for this game - it won't work otherwise

  • Right Analogue Stick - Move Robot
  • A - Jump
  • Left Trigger - Eat scrap
  • Left Bumper (Hold down) - Compress all current scrap into heavy box
  • Right Trigger - Fire current scrap

Two player game - eat and shoot scrap or boxes to knock your opponent into obstacles and deadly killboxes!
