Game Jam 3 - March 2018

We ran our third Game Jam in March 2018. A whopping 8 games were made overall and we're all super proud of the quality of them. Well done everyone!

The theme for this game jam was: Salvage

There were two prizes this year: the main prize for the overall best game, and The Easter Egg Award for the entry that best followed some of the constraints on our Challenges page.

Check out the games on our games page!
