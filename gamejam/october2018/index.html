Game Jam 4 - October 2018

We ran our fourth Game Jam in October 2018. A total of 9 incredibly cool games were made - most of them in 3D!

The theme this jam was: Deceit

There were three prizes this year: the main prize for the overall best game, a second place prize, and The Easter Egg Award for the entry that best followed some of the constraints on our Challenges page.
