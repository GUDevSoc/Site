Game Jam V - February 2019

We ran our fifth game jam in February 2019. We had a huge variety of games this year - from oddball grappling hook spaceship racing games to insanely polished escort mission puzzle games. We super enjoyed playing them, and we hope you will too!

The theme for this game jam was: Twins

There were two prizes this year: the main prize for the overall best game, and The Jam Award for the entry that best followed some of the constraints on our Challenges page.

Check out the games on our games page!
