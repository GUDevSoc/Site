October 2017 Asset Jam

During our asset jam we provided members with two basic games: a shoot-em-up and a platformer.

The games had no art or sound, and we asked attendees to create these assets however they liked, and put them into the game.

Here is what they came up with:

  • Desert Game
  • Student Crying Simulator
  • Cyclops Cyber Elephant
  • Space Cat
